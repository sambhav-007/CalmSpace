What Is CalmSpace?

CalmSpace is an AI-powered journaling companion designed to help you reflect on your thoughts, emotions, and daily experiences in a safe, private space. Powered by advanced AI technologies, it encourages mindfulness and provides calming, reflective insights to promote mental well-being.
