1. Introduction

Welcome to CalmSpace ("we," "our," or "us"). We understand the importance of your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI therapist service.

By accessing or using CalmSpace, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our service:

  • Personal Information: This includes information by which you may be personally identified, such as name, email address, and payment information.
  • Mental Health Information: CalmSpace collects information about your mental health status, concerns, goals, and therapy conversations to provide personalized therapeutic support.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our service, including your interactions with our AI therapist, time spent on the platform, features used, and technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • User-Generated Content: Any content that you voluntarily provide during conversations with our AI therapist.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Direct Interactions: Information you provide when you register for an account, fill out forms, or engage with our AI therapist.
  • Automated Technologies: As you navigate through our service, we may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect usage information.
  • Third-Party Sources: We may receive information about you from third-party service providers, such as payment processors, but only with your consent.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our AI therapy services
  • To personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your mental health needs
  • To train and enhance our AI models (using de-identified data)
  • To process your subscription and manage your account
  • To communicate with you about service updates, features, and support
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Special Note on Mental Health Data

We understand the highly sensitive nature of mental health information. While CalmSpace is not a covered entity under HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act), we voluntarily implement many HIPAA-inspired security measures to protect your mental health information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all therapy conversations
  • Strict access controls to limit employee access to your data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • De-identification of data used for AI training purposes

Please note that CalmSpace is an AI-powered therapeutic support tool and not a replacement for professional mental health treatment. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, and hosting services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

Important: We do NOT sell or rent your personal information or therapy conversations to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Risk of Harm and Crisis Situations

While we respect your privacy, the safety of our users is our highest priority. In rare cases where we have reasonable belief that there is a risk of imminent harm to you or others based on your interactions with our AI therapist, we may contact emergency services or designated emergency contacts.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. However, we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Therapy conversation logs are automatically deleted after 2 years unless you choose to preserve them longer.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to restrict or object to the processing of your information
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

10. Children's Privacy

CalmSpace is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from or about a child, please contact us.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ. If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data to the United States and process it there.

12. Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

13. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website prior to the changes becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.

14. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, California law may provide you with additional rights regarding our use of your personal information. To learn more about your California privacy rights, visit our California Privacy Rights page.
